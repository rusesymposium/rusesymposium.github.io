Practical information

Venue

The RUSE symposium will take place on the University of Manchester’s Sackville Street Campus in the heart of Manchester city centre, just a 5-minute walk from Piccadilly train station or a 10-minute walk from Oxford Road station.

Registration and fees

Registration opens in June. Fees are as follows and include both days of the symposium, with a buffet lunch and refreshments throughout the day. We reserve a limited number of places for PhD students with a subsidized fee through a separate application procedure, details of which will be made available once registration opens.

  • Ordinary fee: £60
  • PhD student fee (upon successful application): £30

A symposium dinner will be arranged for the evening of August 20, charged separately.

Organizing committee

The organizing committee is at your disposal and will answer your questions at rusesymposium@gmail.com.
