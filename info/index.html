Practical information

Registration and fees

N.B. Due to a technical issue, the eStore link below says the conference is “fully booked”. This is not the case. If you wish to attend the conference, please do show up – we will sort the technical issue ASAP, at which point you will be able to pay the fee. Our apologies for the inconvenience.

Register here.

Fees are as follows and include both days of the symposium, with a buffet lunch and refreshments throughout the day. We reserve a limited number of places for PhD students with a subsidized fee through a separate application procedure. To qualify for the reduced rate, please send an email to rusesymposium@gmail.com and attach proof of your student status (e.g. a scan of your student ID card).

  • Ordinary fee: £60
  • PhD student fee (upon successful application): £30

A symposium dinner will be arranged for the evening of August 20, charged separately.

Venue

The RUSE symposium will take place at the Manchester Meeting Place, located on the University of Manchester’s Sackville Street Campus in the heart of Manchester city centre, just a 5-minute walk from Piccadilly train station or a 10-minute walk from Oxford Road station.

Organizing committee

The organizing committee is at your disposal and will answer your questions at rusesymposium@gmail.com.
